October 30th, 2025

A Letter from the Mayor of Quokkaville

It brings me great joy to welcome you to the brand-new Quokkaville website — our digital home and a window into the heart of our smiling town. This project has been in the works for some time, and I couldn’t be prouder to finally share it with you.

Here, you’ll find the latest news and cheerful updates from The Daily Smile, our town’s own newspaper dedicated to celebrating the everyday magic of Quokkaville. You can also look forward to Smiles Monthly, a newsletter that shines a light on local stories, upcoming events, and the friendly faces who make our community so special.

And don’t miss the We Are The Residents section — a growing collection of personal biographies that remind us who we are when we stand together: curious, kind, and forever optimistic.

This website is more than pixels and pages; it’s a reflection of our shared spirit. May it connect us more deeply, inspire us to keep smiling, and remind us that every good story begins right here at home.


Mayor Quincy McQuokka,
Mayor of Quokkaville
