The Quokkaville Hop

A Nursery Rhyme by Sasha McQuokka (age 7)

Hop, hop, hop, the quokkas play,
In the sunshine throughout the day.
Nibble leaves and chase a bee,
Wiggle tails so happily!
Bounce, bounce, bounce, across the sand,
Little paws and tiny hands.
Laughing under skies so blue,
Quokkas love to play with you!

My Snack Song

A Song by Rosie McQuokka (age 4)

I like crunchies in my pocket,
and raisins in my hat.
Sometimes they roll away from me—
but I don’t mind that!
Banana chips go crinkle-crunch,
and smell like sunny days.
Crackers stack up tall and neat,
’till Sasha steals a few away!
I’ve got apples sliced like smiles,
and tiny cubes of cheese.
When I share them with my friends,
they giggle, “More, please!”
Snacks make walking extra fun,
I never leave without.
’Cause snacks are like adventures—
you never run out.

My Flowery Garden

A Short Poem by Sasha McQuokka (age 7)

My flowery garden goes zig-zag-zoo,
With wiggly-waggly flowers of every hue.
Buzzy-buzz bees go bumble-bum-bum,
And tickly butterflies go flap-flap-fum!
I jump in puddles of petal soup,
And do the flower-dance-loopity-loop.
Sniff-sniff-snuffle, oh wow, so sweet!
My garden is the prettiest treat!

My Name is Rosie McQuokka in Letters

An Acrostic Poem by Rosie McQuokka (age 4)

Run-run-run, I’m super fast!
Oopsie-daisy, I fell on grass!
Snack-snack-snack, I love to eat,
I’m twirly-whirly on my feet!
Eek! A bug! But it’s my friend,
My giggles never end!
Catch me, catch me, let’s all play,
Quick-quick-quick, I zoom away!
Under the tree, I hidey-hide,
Oh look! A flower! I sniff-sniff-snide!
Kites fly high, I jump-jump-jump,
Kaboom! I fall in a flower-pump!
Ahahaha! That’s me, Rosie McQuokka!

Queen Alice the Sweet

A Haiku by Finnegan Longtail

Queen Alice rules sweet,
crown of honey, wings that shine--
Buzzes hearts to cheer.

Alice's Sonnet

A Shakespearean Sonnet by Finnegan Longtail

In gardens where the wildflowers bloom,
The queen of sweets begins her flight.
With golden crown and wings of light,
She dances through the sunlit room.

Her laughter rings, a melody,
That lifts the spirits, warms the heart.
In every soul, she plays a part,
a symphony of glee.

Oh, Alice, with your gentle grace,
You turn the world to sweet delight.
Your kindness shines, a radiant light,
In every smile, we see your face.

Forever may your reign extend,
Queen Alice, sweetest of them all.
